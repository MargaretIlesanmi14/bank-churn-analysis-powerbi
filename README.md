# bank-churn-analysis-powerbi

## Key Findings:
. Most churners are 39 to 64 years old and categorized as young professionals, Mid-career, and Pre-retirement customers.
. The overall demographics of the bank's customers show:
-       Age distribution: 29 to 39 years old
-       Tenure distribution: 3 to 7 years
-       Number of product distribution: 1 or 2 products
-       Credit score distribution: 579 to 739
-       Exited distribution: Smaller proportion of customers have churned
. French customers have more balances compared to German and Spanish customers.
. Segments within the bank's customers include: Age group, Tenure, and Gender.

## Recommendations:
路 The bank should improve on their overall customer experience by providing better services, seamless transacting, faster response times, and more personalized interactions.
路 Offer incentives that reward long-term customers and provide incentives to customers at risk of churning.
路 Regularly communicate with customers to understand their needs and concerns, and use feedback to make continuous improvements.
路 Develop a product tailored to suit churners between 39 to 64 years old, categorized as young professionals, Mid-career, and Pre-retirement customers, to help with customer retention.

## Conclusion
I hope this project provides valuable insights for financial institutions and organizations looking to reduce customer churn and improve customer retention.
